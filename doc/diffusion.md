# Diffusion and mass flux

We hear a lot about the turbulence or the moisture mixing drying the cloud layer.
This process would occur in days, and not be representative of a climate equilibrium.

In an equilibrium climate, the moisture profile adjusts so that the fluxes of moisture balance.
The mass flux representation of the cumulus moisture flux can be written as a diffusive flux:
$$
F_{Cu} = M\Delta q = -\mu (\partial q / \partial z).
$$
